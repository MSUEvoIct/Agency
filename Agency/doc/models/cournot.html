Agency: Cournot Oligopoly Model

General

As the name suggests, this model implements the standard Cournot oligopoly model from economics. Each simulation is of exactly one market and exactly two firms, which simultaneously determine output quantity. The market price is determined by using the traditional equilibrium analysis and virtual "walrasian auctioneer" rather than an explicit procurement process (Tesfatsion 2006)
